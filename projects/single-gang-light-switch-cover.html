Single Gang Light Switch Cover

A clean, minimal single-gang switch cover plate, available with a decora/rocker-style cutout or as a blank cover (no cutout) for unused or decorative switches. Each version includes a standard size and a slightly oversized "Large" version (+0.5cm on all sides) for covering paint buildup, chipped edges, or slightly oversized wall openings. Printed with the back face flat against the print bed for the best finish on the visible portion of the covers. Settings: 15–20% grid infill, 0.16mm layer height, 0.2mm first layer height, 2 perimeters, honeycomb auto-generated supports. Designed in SolidWorks.
