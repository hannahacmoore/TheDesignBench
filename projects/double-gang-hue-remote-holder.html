Double Gang Philips Hue Remote Holder Wall Plate

A double-gang wall plate combining a Philips Hue Smart Button/Dimmer Remote holder on one side with either a blank panel or a Decora/rocker cutout on the other, useful for mounting a Hue remote right next to an existing switch, or on its own with a blank second gang. Available in standard and oversized (+0.5cm all sides) versions. Need the holder on the opposite side? Don't rotate the part, the holder's shape is directional, so a 180° rotation points it the wrong way. Use your slicer's Mirror tool instead (in PrusaSlicer: right-click the model, click Mirror, then choose an axis), try the X-axis first, and if it still faces the same way, undo and try the Y-axis. Designed in SolidWorks.
