About

I'm a biomedical engineering student who loves to design in my free time. The Design Bench is where I share the parts, prototypes, and side projects that come out of these designs. I share designs I created to solve real problems, then model, print, or cut so someone else can use them too.

Most work here moves through SolidWorks, Fusion 360, or Onshape depending on the project, and ends up produced by 3D printing (FDM) or laser cutting. Expect functional hardware, fixtures, organizers, and the occasional fun little build, designed to not only work well but look good too.
