Triple Gang Philips Hue Remote Holder Wall Plate

A triple-gang wall plate combining a Philips Hue Smart Button/Dimmer Remote holder with blank and/or Decora/rocker cutout sections, in 7 layout combinations, useful for mounting a Hue remote alongside existing switches or blank panels in any arrangement. Available in standard and oversized (+0.5cm all sides) versions. Most combinations also come in Version 1 (standard screw mounts) and Version 2 (exposed screw mounts removed on flat sections), only combinations with a flat section have this option, since that's the only place a screw needs hiding. Need a mirrored layout? Don't rotate the part, the holder's shape is directional, so a 180° rotation points it the wrong way. Use your slicer's Mirror tool instead, try the X-axis first, and if that's not right, undo and try the Y-axis. Designed in SolidWorks.
