Single Gang Light Switch Cover

A clean, minimal single-gang switch cover plate, available with a decora/rocker-style cutout or as a blank cover (no cutout) for unused or decorative switches. Each version includes a standard size and a slightly oversized "Large" version (+0.5cm on all sides) for covering paint buildup, chipped edges, or slightly oversized wall openings. Designed in SolidWorks.
